gosu-tmx

introduction

gosu-tmx is, as the name implies, a TMX map loader for Gosu. Most importantly, it supports an arbitrary number of layers and provides hooks for object group creation so it can be integrated with other libraries built on top of Gosu. Don't believe me? Have a look at this:

tmx-test.rb

Now that I've got your attention, why not help out? There's always room for improvement, and I don't consider the library anywhere near complete. Comments and suggestions are welcome and encouraged.
